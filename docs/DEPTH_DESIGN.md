## 4. Room Design Principles

Each room must:

- Represent a psychological domain
- Contain:
  - 1 ritual
  - 0–1 boss
  - 1 narrative purpose
- Feel distinct in tone

---

## 5. Boss Design Principles

Each boss must:

- Represent a loop, not an enemy
- Ask a confronting question
- Reveal cost of pattern
- Reward awareness, not “victory”

---

## 6. Ritual Design Rules

Each ritual must include:

- grounding
- visualization
- reflection
- safe exit

---

## 7. Quest Philosophy

Quests are:

- small
- real-world
- aligned with archetype
- not grindy

---

## 8. Post-Day 7 Experience

- Library becomes open exploration
- Player chooses direction
- Archetype begins to evolve

---

## 9. What NOT to do

- No random content
- No meaningless rewards
- No grind loops
- No feature bloat
